A single super speciality hospital is defined as a hospital that is primarily and exclusively engaged in the care and treatment of the patients suffering from a specific illness. They offer specialized services to their patients.

ME-SUPER SPECIALITY HOSPITAL

SUPER SPECIALITY HOSPITAL houses the sub-specialities of Cardiac Surgery, Electrophysiology and Pacing, Clinical and Preventive Cardiology and Interventional Cardiology, where an integrated team of cardiac surgeons and cardiologists work in tandem. Back by the latest technology including a hybrid operating suite, the team of Cardiac Surgery doctors has performed a 50,000+ CABG surgeries. The team perform off-pump CABG, robotic heart surgery, and minimally invasive valve and coronary heart bypass techniques.
